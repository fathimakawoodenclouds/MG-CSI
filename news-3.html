Onam Celebration 2024

The campus came alive with the vibrant colors of Onam. Students created a beautiful Pookalam, participated in traditional games, and enjoyed a grand Onasadya, fostering unity and cultural pride.

The celebrations included a traditional 'Thiruvathira' dance and a 'Vadamvali' (tug-of-war) competition. The atmosphere was filled with joy and camaraderie as students from different backgrounds came together to celebrate the festival. Faculty members also joined in the festivities.

A grand 'Onasadya' feast was served to all students and staff, featuring traditional Kerala dishes. The event showcased the rich cultural heritage of the state and promoted unity and harmony among the college community.

General Legal Quiz Competition

A general quiz on legal current affairs and landmark judgments was conducted for the students. The quiz tested their awareness of the legal world and kept them updated with recent developments.

The quiz covered topics ranging from constitutional amendments to recent Supreme Court verdicts. It encouraged students to read legal news and stay informed. Winners received books on legal philosophy as prizes.

Such competitions play a vital role in honing the analytical skills of law students. The participants expressed appreciation for the challenging questions and the opportunity to test their knowledge against their peers.

National Seminar on Intellectual Property Rights

A National Seminar on Intellectual Property Rights was organized to discuss the evolving landscape of IPR laws. Distinguished speakers shed light on patent laws, copyright issues, and the role of IPR in innovation.

The seminar attracted participants from law colleges across the state. Sessions focused on the challenges of protecting digital rights and the future of IPR in the age of AI. Students presented research papers on various aspects of intellectual property.

Experts discussed case studies involving patent infringements and copyright violations. The interactive Q&A sessions allowed students to clarify doubts and gain practical insights into the field of Intellectual Property Rights.
