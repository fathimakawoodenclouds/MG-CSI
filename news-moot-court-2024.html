Annual Moot Court Competition 2024

The Moderator Gladstone MGCSI Institute of Legal Studies (MGCSI Institute of Legal Studies) successfully hosted its Annual Moot Court Competition 2024, bringing together some of the brightest legal minds from leading law schools across the region. This prestigious event has become a cornerstone of our academic calendar, providing students with an invaluable platform to showcase their advocacy skills, legal research capabilities, and courtroom presence.

The competition featured intense rounds of argumentation, with participants demonstrating exceptional knowledge of legal principles and procedures. Each team presented meticulously researched legal arguments before a distinguished panel of judges comprising senior advocates, practicing lawyers, and members of the judiciary. The event not only tested the participants' understanding of law but also their ability to think on their feet and respond to challenging questions from the bench.

Competition Highlights

The competition witnessed outstanding performances from all participating teams, with each round raising the bar for legal excellence.

  • Over 20 teams from leading law schools participated
  • Distinguished panel of senior advocates and judges
  • Comprehensive legal research and argumentation
  • Real-world case scenarios and complex legal issues

The winning team demonstrated exceptional legal acumen, combining thorough research with persuasive advocacy. Their performance exemplified the high standards of legal education at MGCSI Institute and the practical skills our students develop through such competitions. The event concluded with an awards ceremony where winners were felicitated by the Chief Guest, Hon'ble Justice (Retd.) of the High Court.

This competition provides our students with real-world courtroom experience, helping them develop the confidence and skills necessary to excel in their legal careers.

Dr. Sarah ThomasDean, MGCSI Institute of Legal Studies

The Annual Moot Court Competition continues to be a highlight of our academic program, fostering a culture of excellence in legal education. We congratulate all participants for their dedication and hard work, and we look forward to hosting even more successful editions in the years to come. Such events reinforce our commitment to providing comprehensive legal education that prepares students for the challenges of modern legal practice.
