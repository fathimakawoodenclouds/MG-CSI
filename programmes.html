Admission & Programme Details

Everything you need to know about our programmes

Admission

The criteria for applying to this programme include meeting the eligibility requirements and submitting the application within the specified deadlines. The process is straightforward and transparent, ensuring a smooth admission experience.

Eligibility

Applicants should have completed the prescribed educational qualifications. Certain programmes may have additional requirements to ensure candidates are fully prepared for advanced legal studies.

Application Form

The application form can be submitted online or offline along with the necessary supporting documents.

Upload Form: Download Application Form

Instructions: View Application Instructions

Note: Ensure all documents are properly scanned and uploaded in PDF format.

Important Dates

Key dates include the opening and closing of applications, as well as any scheduled entrance exams or interviews. Students are advised to plan their applications carefully.

Programme Duration

Programmes are structured to provide a complete learning experience. Undergraduate courses typically run for 3-5 years, postgraduate courses for 2 years, and diploma programmes vary depending on the curriculum.

Curriculum / Syllabus

The curriculum includes core legal subjects, practical training, and elective modules designed to enhance both academic understanding and professional readiness.

Fees Structure

A clear and transparent fee structure ensures that students understand the tuition and other applicable charges upfront.

Have Questions?

Contact / Enquiry

For any queries or further information regarding the programmes, students can reach out to the admissions office.
