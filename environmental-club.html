Greener Campus, Better Future

Environmental Club (ECO Club)

What We Do

The Environmental Club, also known as the ECO Club, at Moderator Gladstone MGCSI Institute of Legal Studies is dedicated to promoting environmental awareness and sustainable practices within the campus and beyond. Our mission is to inspire students to become stewards of the environment and advocates for ecological justice.

Through waste management drives, plantation programs, and awareness seminars, we strive to create a greener and more conscious campus community.

Empowering students to lead environmental conservation efforts and foster sustainable living through collective action.

Vision & Mission

Vision:

To create an environmentally sensitive and active student community that leads the way towards a sustainable future.

Mission:

  • Organize awareness campaigns on climate change and conservation
  • Implement green campus initiatives (e.g., plastic-free zones)
  • Partner with local communities for environmental cleanup drives
  • Promote biodiversity through campus garden projects
  • Integrate environmental ethics into legal studies
