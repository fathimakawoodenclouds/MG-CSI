Resources

World-Class Infrastructure

CSI Institute of Legal Studies is equipped with modern facilities and resources designed to support academic excellence, practical learning, and overall student development. Our campus provides a comprehensive environment for education, research, and personal growth.

Our Facilities

Campus Resources

Library

The library supports academic learning and research by providing access to reference materials, study resources, and reading facilities. It offers an organized environment for independent study and academic consultation.

Moot Court Hall

The moot court hall is designed to support practical learning through simulated proceedings and structured academic activities. It provides a formal setting for skill development and academic practice.

Board Room

The board room serves as a space for academic meetings, discussions, and institutional activities. It supports administrative and academic coordination in a professional environment.

Auditorium

The auditorium is used for academic gatherings, institutional programs, and educational activities. It is designed to accommodate larger audiences in a structured and organized manner.

Smart Classrooms

Smart classrooms support classroom learning through modern teaching tools and digital facilities. These spaces are designed to enhance understanding and engagement during academic sessions.

Language Lab

The language lab supports skill development through structured learning tools and guided practice. It assists in improving communication and language proficiency.

Chapel

The chapel provides a calm and reflective space within the campus. It is maintained for quiet reflection and institutional gatherings as required.

College Bus

Transportation facilities are provided to support daily commuting needs. The service is managed to ensure accessibility and convenience for students and staff.

Canteen

The canteen offers refreshment and dining facilities within the campus. It provides a functional space for meals and short breaks during the academic day.

Gym

The gym supports physical well-being by providing facilities for regular fitness activities. It encourages a balanced academic and campus life.

Comprehensive Campus Facilities

Our campus is designed to provide students with everything they need for a well-rounded educational experience:

  • Modern academic infrastructure for effective learning
  • Practical training facilities for skill development
  • Recreational and wellness amenities
  • Comfortable spaces for study and collaboration
  • Transportation and dining services
  • Spiritual and reflective spaces

CSI Institute of Legal Studies is dedicated to providing excellence in legal education, combining academic rigor with practical exposure to shape future legal professionals.
