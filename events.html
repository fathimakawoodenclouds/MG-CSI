Academic & Professional Engagement

Events

Knowledge Sharing & Professional Growth

Seminars, lectures, and various events are regularly organized to promote knowledge sharing, professional growth, and academic engagement. These gatherings offer students and faculty opportunities to learn from experts, discuss emerging topics, and connect with the broader academic and professional community.

What We Organize

Types of Events

Seminars

Interactive sessions featuring expert speakers on contemporary legal topics, industry trends, and academic research.

Guest Lectures

Presentations by distinguished legal professionals, judges, and academicians sharing their expertise and experiences.

Workshops

Hands-on training sessions focused on practical skills, legal drafting, research methodologies, and professional development.

Panel Discussions

Multi-perspective debates and discussions on current legal issues, policy matters, and emerging challenges.

Annual Programs

Special institutional events, celebrations, and academic gatherings that bring together the entire CSI community.

Competitions

Moot courts, debates, and other competitive events that challenge students and enhance their skills.
