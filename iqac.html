Quality Assurance & Continuous Improvement

Internal Quality Assurance Cell

About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) ensures continuous improvement in academics, administration, and overall institutional quality. It promotes a learner-centric environment, encourages feedback-based improvements, and organizes workshops and development programs to enhance performance.

IQAC serves as a catalyst for quality enhancement initiatives and plays a crucial role in maintaining and improving the standards of education and institutional operations.

Promoting a culture of continuous quality enhancement through systematic monitoring and improvement.
Our Goals

Objectives

Quality Culture

Foster a culture of quality in teaching, learning, and administration across all institutional activities and processes.

Innovation & Best Practices

Support innovation and promote the adoption of best practices in academic and administrative functions.

Feedback-Driven Improvements

Promote feedback-driven improvements by collecting, analyzing, and implementing stakeholder suggestions.

Impact of IQAC

IQAC strengthens institutional performance and promotes a culture of continuous quality enhancement.

Key Contributions:

  • Enhanced teaching-learning processes
  • Improved administrative efficiency
  • Better stakeholder satisfaction
  • Systematic quality monitoring
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Data-driven decision making
Continuous Improvement
What We Do

IQAC Activities

Quality Monitoring

Regular monitoring and evaluation of academic and administrative processes to ensure adherence to quality standards.

Workshops & Training

Organization of workshops, seminars, and development programs for faculty and staff to enhance skills and knowledge.

Feedback Collection

Systematic collection and analysis of feedback from students, faculty, and other stakeholders for continuous improvement.

Documentation & Reporting

Preparation of quality reports, documentation of best practices, and maintenance of quality assurance records.
