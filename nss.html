Not Me But You

National Service Scheme (NSS)

Overview

The National Service Scheme (NSS) unit at Moderator Gladstone MGCSI Institute of Legal Studies is a vibrant platform for students to engage in community service and social responsibility. Our unit aims to develop the personality of students through community service, fostering a sense of social and civic responsibility.

Through various activities and camps, our volunteers gain valuable experience in leadership, teamwork, and social problem-solving, all while upholding the NSS motto: "Not Me But You".

Building character through community service and fostering social consciousness among future legal professionals.

Objectives & Moto

Moto:

"Not Me But You" — This reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for selfless service.

Key Objectives:

  • Understand the community in which they work
  • Identify the needs and problems of the community
  • Develop a sense of social and civic responsibility
  • Acquire leadership qualities and democratic attitudes
  • Practise national integration and social harmony
